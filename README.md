# Digital-Healthcare
TA0035 - Khushwant Kongre

## Unified Smart Health Ecosystem
### Doctor • Patient • Pharmacy Connected Platform

## Problem Statement

## Currently in healthcare:

##  - Doctors give prescriptions
##  - Patients buy medicines from pharmacies
##  - Pharmacies maintain separate billing systems
##  - No centralized tracking of medicine purchase
##  - No monitoring of course completion
##  - Risk of duplicate medicine purchases
##  - Drug misuse is difficult to track

## Medical data is fragmented and unsafe.

## Solution

##  Unified Smart Health Ecosystem is a connected digital health platform that synchronizes:

##    -  Doctor
##    -  Pharmacy
##    -  Patient

## All prescription and purchase data is connected in one secure system.

## System Workflow

##    1. Doctor creates a digital prescription.
##    2. System generates a unique QR code.
##    3. Patient visits pharmacy.
##    4. Pharmacist verifies prescription.
##    5. Medicine sale is recorded.
##    6. Patient medical history updates automatically.

## Security Features

##    - Private / Public data visibility option
##    - OTP-based secure sharing
##    - Role-based access control
##    - Encrypted prescription data (simulated)
##    -  Controlled access to patient records

##  If patient keeps data in **Private Mode**:
##  - No one can view records without OTP verification.

##  If patient switches to **Public Mode**:
##  - Records can be viewed directly.
